About Me


In 2018 I began my career in wrestling media. Alongside my co-host and friend Emelia we launched Kayfabe Queens as an outlet for our passion for all things professional wrestling from the perspective of two latinx women. Over the course of the past 3 years and nearly 50 episodes we have carved out a space for ourselves in a traditionally male dominated field. We have been fortunate enough to have a diverse mix of people in the wrestling community on from wrestlers to influencers, writers,other podcasters, and more on our show!

In 2022 I branched out and joined Women's Wrestling Talk, a women founded wrestling media network. I began working on their social media and hosting 2 YouTube shows; Turnbuckle Glam and AEW Dynamite aftershow while also appearing on other shows on the network.

In 2022 I also began taking coding classes at Bitwise as a way to chalenge myself and gain new skills. I created a website for Kayfabe Queens. These classes helped me grow a deeper understanding of technology and media which have always been passions,now I get to combine them for my ultimate passion wrestling.
